    1. The contest is open for online submissions only.Any individual can participate in the competition. The entries should be mailed to pixelate@dhishna.org

    3. Submissions will not be accepted once the deadline lapses (5th Of January).
    4. Photos submitted must be in JPEG format.
    5. Only one entry is allowed per participant.
    6. You are required to provide a unique title & description for your entry.
    7. Submitted photo should be the original work of the participant,any conflict in ownership shall lead to disqualification. However border(s), logo(s), copyright marks, or any other visible references and/or marks on the image is not allowed. EXIF data should be produced by the contestant on request.

    12.By participating, you authorise TreeMonks to use your submission on their social media handles.
    13.The initial filtering will be done by the judges and the selected photographs will be announced through the official media pages of TreeMonks .
